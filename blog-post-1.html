Introducing Project Connect

A New Model for Open Source Trading Communities

PC
Project Connect Team

January 1, 2025

5 min read Announcements 0 comments

Today, we're excited to announce the launch of Project Connect, an ambitious initiative to build viral, monetized open-source teams with equitable remuneration for all contributors.

The Problem We're Solving

Traditional open-source projects face significant challenges:

  • Sustainability: Most projects struggle with long-term funding and maintenance
  • Contributor Retention: Talented contributors often leave for paid opportunities
  • Equitable Rewards: Contributions are rarely financially rewarded proportionally
  • Community Growth: Building and maintaining vibrant communities is difficult

Our Solution

Project Connect introduces a revolutionary model that combines:

Viral Growth

Exceptional community building strategies

Monetization

Sustainable revenue streams

Equitable Rewards

Fair compensation for all contributors

Thriving Communities

Inclusive, diverse communities

Our Trading Journal Platform

As our first implementation, we're building a comprehensive trading journal platform that:

  • Helps traders track performance with advanced analytics
  • Offers skill-based challenges with real rewards
  • Provides equitable revenue sharing for top performers
  • Builds a community of like-minded traders

How You Can Get Involved

Whether you're a developer, trader, educator, or community builder, there are multiple ways to contribute:

Developers

Help build our platform

Traders

Use our journal and participate in challenges

Community

Help build our contributor community

Join Us

We believe that open-source collaboration can be both sustainable and rewarding for everyone involved. Project Connect is our attempt to prove this model can work at scale.

If you're interested in being part of this journey, we'd love to have you join our community:
