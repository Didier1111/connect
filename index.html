Our Vision

Traditional open-source projects struggle with sustainability and contributor retention. Project Connect is developing a replicable model that solves these issues by creating communities that naturally attract and retain contributors, implementing sustainable revenue streams, and ensuring all contributors are fairly compensated for their work.

How It Works

1. Community Building

We've developed strategies for creating viral growth in open-source communities, focusing on inclusivity, mentorship, and meaningful contribution opportunities.

2. Monetization Framework

Our model includes multiple revenue streams such as open core, SaaS offerings, support & consulting, enterprise licensing, and community-driven funding.

3. Equitable Remuneration

We use transparent contribution tracking and fair revenue sharing models to ensure all contributors are appropriately rewarded for their work.
