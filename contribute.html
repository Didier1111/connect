Why Contribute?

Contributing to Project Connect offers unique benefits and opportunities.

Equitable Revenue Share

Earn a share of platform revenue based on your contributions. Top contributors can earn 5-20% revenue share.

Skill Development

Enhance your technical and trading skills while working on real-world projects with experienced professionals.

Professional Network

Connect with like-minded professionals and build relationships that can advance your career.

Recognition & Achievement

Gain recognition for your contributions through our achievement system and community spotlight.

Career Opportunities

Build a portfolio of work and gain experience that can lead to job opportunities in trading and tech.

Meaningful Impact

Be part of creating a more equitable and sustainable model for open-source collaboration.

Revenue Sharing Tiers

Earn significant rewards based on your contributions and performance

Bronze: 5% Share Silver: 10% Share Gold: 15% Share Platinum: 20% Share

Challenge Prizes

Compete in skill-based challenges with cash prizes

  • Consistency Challenge: $500 prize pool
  • Risk Management Challenge: $1,000 prize pool
  • Adaptability Challenge: $2,000 prize pool
  • Strategy Mastery Challenge: $5,000+ prize pools

Monthly Bonuses

Additional rewards for top contributors

  • Top 10 Contributors: $100-500 bonuses
  • Community Builders: Special recognition
  • Mentors: Additional rewards
  • Innovators: Patent sharing opportunities
